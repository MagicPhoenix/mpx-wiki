## Trident Assembly Tips

import { Callout } from 'nextra/components'

<Callout type="info">

The kit's packaging is designed by the assembly sequence.  We strongly recommend not taking parts and pieces out of each layer. You can take every layer out but keep them as layers with parts intact.

</Callout>

<Callout type="info">

These Assembly Tips are specific to the Trident. You will also need to refer to the [General Assembly Tips](../GENERAL-KIT/Assembly-Tips.mdx) that are common to the V2.4 and the Trident. The V2.4-specific assembly tips are [here](../VORON-2.4-CBT-KIT/Assembly-Tips.mdx).

</Callout>
