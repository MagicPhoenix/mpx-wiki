### Cable Gland

The standard cable gland will come with a m12 nut, we will use it instead of printed threads.

![image-20231107145319356](https://img.mpx.wiki/i/2023/11/07/6549dee31c6fe.webp)

If you have a steel wire, just go through the gland and tighten the nut, it should be able to hold it there. 
 

## Front Steppers

Currently there are two ways to wire it, one on the side of Y extrusions, the other way on top of Y extrusions. 

### On the side of Y extrusions

Print `wiring_side_x2.stl`

If using this way,you need to make side panels foam thicker, 6mm would be fine.(standard voron is 3mm)
