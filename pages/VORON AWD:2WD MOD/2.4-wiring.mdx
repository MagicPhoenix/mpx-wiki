#### Cable Gland

The standard cable gland will come with a m12 nut, we will use it instead printed thread.

![image-20231107145319356](https://img.mpx.wiki/i/2023/11/07/6549dee31c6fe.webp)

If you have a steel wire, just go through the gland and tight nut, it should be able to hold it there. 

 

### Front Steppers

Currently there's two way to wire it, one by side of Y extrusions, another way by top of Y extrusions. 

#### By side of Y extrusions

Print `wiring_side_x2.stl`

In this way you need to make side panels foam thicker, 6mm would be fine.(standard voron would be 3mm)
