# Trident AWD/2WD Manual


## Extrusion Cut

![image-20231229222537887](https://img.mpx.wiki/i/2023/12/29/658ed6f6a3cc2.webp)

![image-20231229230425559](https://img.mpx.wiki/i/2023/12/29/658edffe3a401.webp)



As 9mm belts are extended a bit, the rear extrusion needs to be filed like this:

![image-20231229222706368](https://img.mpx.wiki/i/2023/12/29/658ed73c60c83.webp)



We made a printed jig for this, you can download it on github:

https://github.com/MagicPhoenix/MPX-VORON-AWD/blob/main/Printed%20Parts/Trident/Extrusion_cut_jig.stl

![image-20231229222746534](https://img.mpx.wiki/i/2023/12/29/658ed76421200.webp) 

Another way is just cut 2.5mm short.

![image-20231229224402003](https://img.mpx.wiki/i/2023/12/29/658edb3425a53.webp)

The 2.5x4mm area is what needs to be cut off.

You also need different printed brackets, download here:

 https://github.com/MagicPhoenix/MPX-VORON-AWD/tree/main/Printed%20Parts/Trident
